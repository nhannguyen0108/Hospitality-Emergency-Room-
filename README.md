# Hospitality-Emergency-Room-Dashboard

## Project Overview
The Hospital Emergency Room Dashboard is a Business Intelligence project developed in Power BI to analyze Emergency Room (ER) operations and monitor key performance indicators (KPIs). The dashboard provides healthcare stakeholders with interactive insights into patient flow, waiting time, service quality, and departmental referrals, enabling data-driven decisions to improve operational efficiency and patient care. The project follows a complete BI workflow, including business requirement analysis, data cleaning, data modeling, DAX calculations, dashboard development, and insight generation.

## Business Objectives
The dashboard aims to:

- Monitor daily Emergency Room patient volume.
- Measure average patient waiting time.
- Evaluate patient satisfaction.
- Analyze department referral patterns.
- Track the percentage of patients seen within 30 minutes (Timeliness KPI).
- Support operational planning through interactive reporting and performance analysis.

## Tools and Technologies
- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Microsoft Excel(.csv)
- Data Modeling (Star Schema)
- Power BI Service (Optional)

## Dashboard Pages
1. Monthly View
> Provides a monthly operational overview of the Emergency Room, including:

- Number of Patients
- Average Wait Time
- Patient Satisfaction Score
- Admission Status
- Patient Age Distribution
- Department Referrals
- Timeliness (% of patients seen within 30 minutes)
- Gender Distribution
- Race Distribution
- Patient Volume by Hour and Day
